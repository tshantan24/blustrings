About Us

BluStrings is a creation of the 21st Century, bringing in experience from different fields of science, communication and marketing; we offer a very niche end-product to our clients.

Having been in the industry and sharing 18+ man-years of experience, we believe in our commitment to guarantee and deliver a successful event with seamless execution; providing a mix of professional services and creativity. We have created, managed & executed a plethora of events across the board.

With a dedicated team that covers all avenues in building your project right from the ground up, we conceptualize and develop on comprehensive plans and ideas that suit your budget. Our team is with you at every step of the way.

Event Solutions

BluStrings understands the importance of celebrating an occasion. As the years pass, Social events, Cultural Shows, Music Festivals, Concept Events & Club Shows have only grown in number.
